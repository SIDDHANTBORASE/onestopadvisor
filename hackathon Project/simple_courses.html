Understanding Career Streams

After Class 10 or 12, you can choose from three main streams. Each stream opens different career opportunities:

Science

Core Subjects:
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Mathematics
  • Biology
Career Options:
  • Engineer
  • Doctor
  • Researcher
  • Data Scientist
Available Courses:
B.Tech Duration: 4 years
MBBS Duration: 5.5 years
B.Sc Duration: 3 years

Commerce

Core Subjects:
  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • Mathematics
Career Options:
  • Chartered Accountant
  • Business Analyst
  • Banker
  • Entrepreneur
Available Courses:
B.Com Duration: 3 years
BBA Duration: 3 years

Arts/Humanities

Core Subjects:
  • History
  • Geography
  • Political Science
  • Literature
Career Options:
  • Civil Servant
  • Journalist
  • Teacher
  • Lawyer
Available Courses:
B.A. Duration: 3 years
